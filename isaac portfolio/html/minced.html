Minced Meat with Spaghetti

Ingredients:

  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tomatoes (blended or chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Spaghetti (enough for 2–4 servings)

Instructions:

  1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions until golden.
  3. Add garlic, cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add minced beef and cook until browned.
  5. Add tomatoes and tomato paste, stir and let it simmer.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper.
  7. Simmer for 10–15 minutes until thick.
  8. Serve over spaghetti and enjoy!
