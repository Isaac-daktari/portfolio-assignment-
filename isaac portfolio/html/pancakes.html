Simple Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, beat the egg, then add milk and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients and stir until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour some batter into the pan and cook until bubbles form.
  6. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite toppings!
